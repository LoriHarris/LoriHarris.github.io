Lori Harris, Data Consultant

Founder and Lead Innovator of Data Play

Lori Harris created Data Play Consulting because she likes to play with data! Its just that simple. She has a passion for exploring trends and connections in data with intellectual curiosity and a dedication for researching until a solution is achieved. After years of being the office "excel nerd", plugging everything into a spreadsheet for all to see, Lori began looking into more programmatic data analytics techniques, which led her into the field of Data Science. She obtained her undgrad degree in Business Administration from UNC-Wilmington, her MBA at Gardner-Webb University, and her Certificate of Data Analytics from UNC-Chapel Hill.
