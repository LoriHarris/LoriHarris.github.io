Weather versus Latitude

Windiness

Wind speeds were varied, however, similar to the humidity trends, when the latitude hit an extreme, so did the wind speed. Also noted, lower wind speeds were more frequently recorded in the latitude ranges from 0 to 75. Again, wind speeds were still quite varied regardless of latitude.
